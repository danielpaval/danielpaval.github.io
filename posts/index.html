Win32 Epoch (1/1/1601)

0001-01-01
ℹ️ This is a quick blog post to mainly play with Jekyll. Not long ago Windows on my laptop reported that Skype was last used on 1/1/1601.. While I rarely use Skype now-a-days, it surely wasn’t that long! January 1st does hint at some kind of a date baseline, just like the Unix epoch, but why 1601? Of course, this was a quick answer to look up and it turns out to be that “1601 was the first year of the 400-year Gregorian calendar cycle at the time Windows NT was made” 1.
